About Me A BRIEF INTRODUCTION OF MYSELF

Aaron Key
Data Engineer • Data Scientist

I developed a fascination for working with data when I looked into how to min max a strategy in an online game. This small passion project and my natural mathematics skills landed me an apprenticeship in the NHS to gain skills in Data Analytics, where I developed a passion for Data Engineering and Data Science.

My key areas of interest lie in statistics and machine learning, and I believe fundamentally that to be successful in a data career, its integral to have a knowledge base in Data Engineering. I also believe that Data Careers are working professionals and have an ambition to support the fostering of professionalisation via organisations like the BCS and RSS.

Key traits I pride myself in are a neverending drive for continued professional development, a thirst for solving complex problems and a goal to provide impactful but easy to understand analysis in driving business decisions
